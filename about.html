About this tool

Why this exists

A lot of students in the program understand their raw marks fine, but get lost translating that into a grade point or CGPA — the official portal shows the result, but not the reasoning behind it. This tool exists to close that gap: same result, explained the way the handbook actually defines it.

What this is not

It's not an official BOU service, doesn't replace the official portal, and isn't affiliated with the university. Nothing here should be treated as the authoritative record of your result — always confirm anything important against the official site or your registrar.

What data this uses, and what it doesn't do

This tool looks up one student's result at a time, by the exact ID entered — the same way the official portal works. It doesn't publish a browsable list of students, doesn't rank or compare students against each other, and doesn't display anyone's result without their own ID being entered first.

This is an independent, unofficial companion tool — not run or endorsed by Bangladesh Open University.
